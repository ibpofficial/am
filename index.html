Squad Protocols & Rules

Official Rulebook

The Gaddar Index (Daily Judgment)

  • Every member can vote daily to accuse someone of being a "Gaddar" (Traitor).
  • An accusation requires exactly 3 Squad Approvals to be confirmed.
  • MANDATORY PROOF: You cannot approve an accusation without typing a detailed reason/proof in the Tribunal.
  • Once confirmed, the target suffers a +10% penalty to their Gaddar Index.
  • If a member's Gaddari Index exceeds 70%, their voting and weapon privileges are immediately revoked until they appeal and clear their name.

Andi Mandi Cards (Tribunal Requests)

  • Requests to use a Satvik, Lahsun, or Non-Veg card must pass through the Tribunal.
  • All card usage requests require exactly 3 Squad Approvals with mandatory written proof.
  • WEEKLY RESET: Every Monday, all players' Satvik cards are automatically renewed/reset to exactly 2.
  • INACTIVITY PENALTY: If you do not open your account for 3 continuous days, your Satvik cards will be wiped to 0 for the remainder of the week.

Defense Shields & Weapons Economy

  • You must trade exactly 1 Satvik Card to buy 1 Defense Shield.
  • TIME LIMIT: Shields can ONLY be purchased on Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday.
  • UNDO WINDOW: You can only "Undo" (sell) a shield to get your card back within 30 seconds of buying it.
  • Shields automatically block 1 confirmed Gaddari penalty.

Neta Alliances

  • You can propose a Neta Alliance with any other member.
  • Allied members can easily transfer Satvik and Lahsun cards to each other to help out.
  • To forcefully break an alliance early, a member must sacrifice exactly 1 Non-Veg Card.

Tribunal Transparency & Overlords

  • Full Transparency: All Tribunal cases now publicly display exactly who voted to approve/reject and what proof they provided.
  • Auto-Resolution: Any case left pending in the Tribunal for 48 hours is automatically resolved based on the current majority of votes.
  • Overlords can edit any operative's inventory, force system announcements, and instantly wipe servers.
